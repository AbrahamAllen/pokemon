welcome to the tutorial
click anywhere on the map to move there
click on your monster to see your stats
move inside the animated biomes to find other monsters
click on any of the attacks in the bottom right to use them
attacks have a base power and an accuracey rating out of 100
careful! the enemy will attack back if you dont kill them
running away will always work!
you can swap between monsters without using your turn
beating an enemy allows you to eat them to heal, absorb them to grow stronger or capture them to add to your party
the biomes will shrink as you find monsters in them
eventually you wont be able to find monsters anymore
some elements are more effective against others
like water is great against fire!
when youre ready, head to the portal to start the real game.
youll be picking your own starter animal and element
if you can get to the 10th floor theres a strong boss waiting for you.
